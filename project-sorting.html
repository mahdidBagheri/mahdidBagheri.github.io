Real-Time Agricultural Sorting

YOLO TensorRT Knowledge Distillation Edge AI

This project involved developing a high-throughput Minimum Viable Product (MVP) integrating Computer Vision with mechanical actuation. The system achieved 95% accuracy at a capacity of 250 kg/h.

MVP demonstration: Falling Sorters in action

Optimization Pipeline

To achieve real-time performance on edge devices, I proposed a semi-supervised knowledge distillation pipeline. This involved training a large teacher model (EfficientNet/DINOv2) and distilling knowledge into a lightweight student model (YOLO).
